About Me

I am currently enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp at the University of Texas, in Austin, TX. This is my site.

Since June of 2016, I have been working as an Email Production Consultant for Salesforce.com. My responsibilities consist of creating HTML paste emails utilizing HTML, CSS, and Ampscript. Leveraging Salesforce Tools and Systems, I have worked to update content and script email personalization.

Before joining Salesforce.com, I worked at FTD. I started as a Web Operations Coordinator, working with the IT and marketing teams to implement website changes. After 8 months, I was promoted to Marketing Associate.

I graduated from West Virginia University with a Master of Science degree in Integrated Marketing Communications. I spent my time analyzing different company campaigns, building proposals, researching demographics, and calculating the effectiveness of current campaigns. I have studied the inner workings of marketing from all different aspects and know what it takes to pull off highly effective campaign; from customer experiences to social media.
